Glass waste

Glass waste is any abandoned or discarded glass item that has either run its course or is no longer useful. Glass bottles, jars, windows, and other broken or surplus glassware are common examples of glass waste. There are enormous volumes of waste glass powder created worldwide of the 200 million tons of solid garbage, 7% were glass waste (Muhedin & Ibrahim, 2023). Glass waste makes up a sizable portion of municipal solid waste and, if improperly managed, can have negative effects on the environment.
