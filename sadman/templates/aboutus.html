Quick Shop Save Time

Quick shop save Time is an Bangladeshi e-commerce company, headquartered in Dhaka, and incorporated in Singapore as a private limited company. The company initially focused on online book sales before expanding into other product categories such as consumer electronics, fashion, home essentials, groceries, and lifestyle products. The service competes primarily with Amazon India and domestic rival Snapdeal.[5][6]
