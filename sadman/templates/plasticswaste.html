plastic waste

Plastic waste is any plastic material or product that has been discarded or left unused after performing its original function and is no longer needed. Over the years, the harmful effects of plastic pollution have been felt by an estimated 700 species of aquatic life (Babaremu et al., 2022). It consists of plastic in a variety of shapes and sizes, including bottles, bags, containers, packing materials, and more. Because it can linger in the environment for hundreds of years and harm ecosystems and species, plastic waste is a serious environmental hazard.
